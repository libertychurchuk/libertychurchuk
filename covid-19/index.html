We together are a people full of faith and full of the Holy Spirit who are adaptable and flexible to not let circumstances be the biggest voice, but rather allow Jesus to be glorified through this season. Here are some practical changes which we are putting in place to help. We want to remind you that you are free and powerful people who are able to make choices and know that we will support you in your decisions, understanding that circumstances are different for every individual and family. We are continuing to monitor [government guidelines](https://www.gov.uk/government/topical-events/coronavirus-covid-19-uk-government-response) on how people can gather. We want to honour what is being said while also understanding what are suggestions and what is law.
