9.30am - Interactive Family Service

This service will be a fun family interactive service which will be aimed at families with kids under 12. You will be seated around your own table as a family with fun activities to do, worship and the Word. Bring your own colouring pens and pencils! It will be a great time for the whole family!

11am - Service

Join us for a great time of worship, hear the Word and spend time together.

11am - Facebook LIVE Service

Our 11am in person service will be streamed on Facebook LIVE if you are unable to join us at this time. Visit www.facebook.com/libertychurchfamily

Registration is needed for our in person services as space is limited. Visit the link below to register.

Due to COVID-19, there will be a number of measures in place at the Gaer Christian Centre to ensure that everyone is safe, in line with government legislation. Social distancing will be adhered to throughout the building. Toilets will be available, we will be operating a 'one in one out' system. Your temperature will be checked on entry and please wear a mask at all times whilst inside the building. If you have COVID-19 symptoms, we kindly ask you to stay at home.

We can't wait to see you there!
